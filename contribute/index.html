Start Contributing

We need information for organization like your own. We gather information from student Organizations through a series of forms and documents, which is then returned to us. From there, we organize and publish the data for everyone to view.

Why Contribute

There are lots of reasons why you should contribute information.

  • Help Others Other organizations need your help to run successfully

  • Gain Notoriety Boast about your best achievements and gain the respect of other organizations.

  • Coming soon, you'll need to. Starting late Spring 2019, we will be restricting access of information only to organizations which contribute sufficient information.

How to Contribute

There are two main methods to contribute information.

Bulk Contribution

For those who need to upload a lot of information - such as first-time uploaders - bulk contriubtion is the way to go. With bulk upload, we will send you a set of excel documents which you will fill out and return to us. This way, you can copy and paste or quickly enter a lot of information into the forms.

To get started, click the "Bulk Contribution" tab to the left.

Single Contribution

For those who only a small amount of information, such as less than 7 entries, to contribute or only wish to update information, single upload the way to go. You'll be directed to fill out a series of airtable forms (like google forms) which will directly enter information into our databases.

To get started, click the "Single Contribution" tab to the left.

First Time

If this is your first time contributing, then you may want to contact us before starting the process. There are several ways to get in contact with us:

  • Attend a Presentation Through the semester, we host introductory sessions where we explain how we work and answer questions from the audience. You can find these events on Facebook when available.

  • Find us in a Council Meeting If your organization is a member of a student council, we may be presenting at some centralized meeting. Be sure to come talk to us then.

  • Fill out the Introductory Form If you're interested in what we do and want to talk to us directly, fill out this introductory form

  • Email longhorndataexchange@gmail.com If all else fails, email us directly at longhorndataexchange@gmail.com
