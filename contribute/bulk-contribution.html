Step 3. Have your officers fill out the relavant forms

Each form is intended for a specific role in your organization. These roles may be the responsibility of a single officer or a group of officers. We ask that you forward the appropriate forms to the appropriate officers.

You can use the following table to determine who should recieve which document:

Exchange Purpose Role
Banquets Assist in the preparation, planning, and foresight that needs to go into organizing a banquet. Banquet planner(s)
Caterers Provide a scaled list of caterers for different events. Officer(s) that order food for events and meetings
Corporate Supporter Provide names and information of corporate supporters. Corporate relations officer(s)
Equipment Allow for organizations to borrow/rent equipment, that would otherwise be more difficult to obtain, through the LDE equipment database and equipment contracts. More information on the contract types are available on the Equipment Exchange document. Note: When using this exchange, only list equipment you organization personally owns, not items that your organization has access to. President, operations vice president, secretary, or any other applicable position(s)
Officer Structure Provide insight so organizations can function efficiently and effectively to meet their goals and needs for their specific missions. Senior leadership officers
Social Provide locations, information, and ideas for social events. Social or events coordinator(s)
Speaker Provide names and various information about speakers for events, including panels, workshops, fireside chats, tours, etc. Academic and corporate relation officer(s)
Technology Provide information on popular technologies and services used by organizations (internally and externally). This includes but is not limited to: officer communication, member communication, sales, inventory, etc. Common examples include: Slack, Facebook Messenger, GroupMe, Snapchat, Instagram, Facebook, Websites, Google Drive, etc. Senior leadership officers
Volunteer Opportunities Provide locations, information, and ideas for volunteering events. Volunteer and outreach organizer(s)

Step 4. Return the forms to us

When the forms are complete, you can send them back to us with the return document form. Anyone in your organization can use this form to submit information.

Once we have processed the forms, we will send you a thank you note and you should be able to see your data on the website. If you need, you can also check what forms we have received from your organization through our current contributors page.
