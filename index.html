Longhorn Data Exchange

We seek to improve student organizations at the University of Texas by using information collected from student organizations to produce insights on options, reviews, and best practices among student organizations. Ethan Freeburg was here.

Running a student organization at UT is hard, as leaders often have to make important decisions with little information. We improve student organizations outcomes by providing more information about the options available to student organizations.

At the same time, we are aware that student organizations know how to function and have lots of experience. We aim to capitalize on this experience by sharing these experiences with other student organizations in a structured and useful manner.
