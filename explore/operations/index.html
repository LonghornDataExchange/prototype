Operation Exchanges

The operation category includes a broad range of topics about what organizations do, including events, technology, training, and officer structures.

The exchanges are listed below:

  • Banquets: Venues, caterers, activities, and prices, to help those planning banquets.

  • Caterers: Caterers, prices, and ratings for those who need to buy food.

  • Corporate Supporters: Company names to help fundraisers and corporate liaisons scope companies to contact.

  • Equipment: Items other organizations have available to be borrowed or rented.

  • Officer Structures: Records detailing officer jobs and relationships, for you to find equivalent officers or compare officer structures.

  • Socials: Venues, price, and ratings of social activities for social planners.

  • Speakers: Speakers, workshops, and firesides held by student organizations for event planners.

  • Technology: Social media platforms, website tools, POS systems, simulation software, and other technologies used by student organizations.

  • Volunteer Opportunities: Organizations, locations, and time frames for service planners.
